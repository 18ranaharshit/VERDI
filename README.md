## 🎯 About

Verdi is a visually stunning, highly interactive web application designed to help students track the environmental impact of their daily commutes. Instead of just showing boring statistics, Verdi gamifies sustainability. Every time you choose to walk, cycle, or take public transit instead of driving, you earn **Carbon Credits**. You can then spend these credits in a beautifully crafted Rewards Marketplace on real campus perks-like free bike unlocks, canteen discounts, or priority library booking! It’s built for anyone who wants to make greener choices while having a blast doing it.

> [!NOTE]  
> Verdi calculates emissions based on actual IPCC-standard carbon factors, meaning your "Carbon Saved" numbers are scientifically accurate!

## 🔄 How It Works

1. 🌍 **Log Your Journey** - Open the beautifully animated Trip Calculator, drop a pin on the interactive map or type your origin and destination.
2. 🚲 **Choose Your Transport** - Select how you traveled (Walk, Cycle, Bus, Car). The Carbon Meter instantly reacts to show your emission levels!
3. 💰 **Earn Carbon Credits** - Hit "Log This Trip". If you chose a green transport mode, you'll instantly see an animated toast rewarding you with Carbon Credits (`CR`) and achievement badges.
4. 🛍️ **Hit the Marketplace** - Browse the Rewards Catalog using your new credits.
5. 🎟️ **Redeem & Scan** - Buy a "Free Campus Bike Unlock", head to your Vouchers tab, and show the live-countdown QR code to the campus attendant to claim your reward!

## ✨ Features

### 🎮 The Gamified Economy
- 🔥 **Carbon Credit Wallet** - A dedicated, premium dashboard tracking your total `CR` balance, credits earned vs. spent, and an infinite-scrolling transaction feed.
- 🔥 **Rewards Marketplace** - An elegant, glassmorphism catalog where you can spend credits on Campus Vouchers (Food, Study, Transport, Store).
- 🔥 **Active Voucher Hub** - Manage your redemptions. Active vouchers feature live countdown timers and scannable QR codes for real-world claiming.

> [!TIP]  
> **The Reward That Powers Itself:** You can use the Carbon Credits you earned from cycling to campus to purchase a "Free Campus Bike Unlock" voucher, enabling you to cycle even more!

### 🗺️ Journey Tracking
- 🔥 **Interactive Route Calculator** - Calculate precise distances and carbon footprints for any route using the interactive Leaflet map.
- 🔥 **Live Carbon Meter** - An animated SVG gauge that dynamically sweeps from green to red based on the transport mode you select.
- 🔥 **Human Equivalents** - Translates your CO₂ footprint into relatable terms (e.g., "equivalent to charging 50 smartphones" or "boiling a kettle 10 times").

### 🏆 Social & Achievements
- 🔥 **Badge Unlock System** - Earn beautifully illustrated achievements for hitting milestones like "5-Day Streak", "100km Cycled", or "First Public Transit".
- 🔥 **Global Leaderboards** - Compete against peers globally or within your specific institution to see who is the ultimate eco-warrior.

> [!TIP]  
> **Scientific Nature UI:** The entire app is draped in a custom "Scientific Nature" design system, utilizing fluid Framer Motion animations, rich emerald and amber gradients, and frosted glass effects optimized for modern mobile devices.

## 🤓 Did You Know?

> [!NOTE]  
> 💬 The Framer Motion animations run at a buttery-smooth 60fps utilizing hardware-accelerated CSS transforms, making the UI feel incredibly snappy even on older phones.

> [!NOTE]  
> 💬 The database logic uses atomic operations (like `$inc`) when awarding Carbon Credits to prevent race conditions if a user logs multiple trips simultaneously!

> [!NOTE]  
> 💬 Active vouchers have an automated background job (`expireVouchers.js`) that runs every 5 minutes on the server to sweep and expire any QR codes that have run out of time.
